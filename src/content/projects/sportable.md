## Overview

During my first year at Imperial College I worked on a project to promote health
and well-being. My team focused on improving the portability of commonly used
sports equipment through prototyping and human-centred design.

My proposal was a collapsible badminton racket that fits easily in a user's bag
without taking up much space. After the necessary research and human-factors
measurements, I produced low-fidelity prototypes to test and refine the concept.
