HostHunt flips the rental marketplace on its head. Instead of guests trawling
endless listings, **guests post what they need** — destination, dates, budget,
must-haves — and **hosts compete with bids** to win the booking. More leverage
for travellers, warmer leads for hosts.

## What I built

A full-stack web app split into three surfaces — **guests, hosts and
moderators** — each with its own dashboard. Guests create stay requests; an
hourly matching service routes them to relevant hosts; hosts bid; the two sides
settle it over in-platform chat. React + TypeScript on the front end, a Node
back end, Firebase for auth, and Stripe / Plaid for payments and identity
verification.

## My role

Founder and lead engineer — product definition, the reverse-bid model, the
data model (guests, hosts, properties, bids, reviews), and the front-end build,
with some contract help on delivery.

## Status

A working concept under active development. The hosted page above shows the
product positioning; the app itself is private while it takes shape.
