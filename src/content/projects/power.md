## Overview

During summer 2017 I decided to attempt making my own power bank. The goal was
to make something universal with a very large capacity — rarely needing to be
charged.

I tried to find complete modules and assemblies wherever possible, since at the
time my manufacturing resources were limited to a soldering iron and a hot glue
gun. The build stacked high-capacity cells with a boost converter and protection
circuitry, wrapped in a custom enclosure with multiple output standards.
