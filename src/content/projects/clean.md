## Overview

My second-year design project explored the environmental impact of FMCG
products. After tearing down donor products, analysing their cradle-to-grave
journey and understanding the product–service system as a whole, we developed a
proposal for a more sustainable solution.

Our solution was to package detergents in a standardised aluminium can. Cans are
14× more recyclable than plastic packaging, and the cylindrical shape is 30% more
space-efficient — further reducing the carbon footprint across transport and
storage.
