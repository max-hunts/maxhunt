How it works

Three steps to a stay that competes for you.

1

Post your stay

Tell hosts where you're headed, your dates, budget and what matters most. One request, no endless scrolling.

2

Hosts bid to win you

Verified hosts with matching properties compete with their best offers — you sit back as the deals roll in.

3

You pick the winner

Compare bids, chat with hosts and book securely. The price you pay is the price you were offered — no surprises.

● You set the terms

Name your budget and dates up front. Hosts work to meet them, not the other way around.

● Verified on both sides

Identity, payment and property checks (KYC, Plaid, Stripe) keep guests and hosts accountable.

● Secure by design

In-platform chat, protected payments and reviews — every stay handled end to end.
